Introduction

Manchester University Sub Aqua Club (MUSAC) is Manchester University's scuba diving society. We offer try dives, training, trips, and socials, have our own equipment, and are affiliated with the British Sub-Aqua Club (BSAC).

We are a friendly and inclusive club, and welcome divers from all agencies. We have a range of members from complete beginners to experienced divers, and we cater for all levels of experience.

For more information, please check out our Facebook  and Instagram  pages or browse our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).
